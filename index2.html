Why this book?

Clarity beats chaos

Open relationships struggle when agreements are fuzzy and check-ins are rare. You don’t need more debates — you need a shared framework.

Tools > theory

When feelings run high, a script or worksheet you can open right now works better than a long essay. This is a playbook, not a manifesto.

Calm, kind structure

Rituals like a 30-minute weekly check-in lower anxiety, build trust, and make renegotiation safe — even with busy lives.

FAQ

Who is this for?

Couples and individuals in — or curious about — ethical non-monogamy. Also helpful for a visiting partner (“the third”). Tools, not theory.

How long do exercises take?

Most tools take 15–30 minutes. Start with one this week; momentum matters more than finishing the whole book.

Is this therapy?

No. It’s a toolkit of agendas, worksheets, and scripts. Many readers also use therapy; the playbook gives shared language and structure.
